Healthier snack substitutions

Enjoy your favorites with simple, tasty swaps that keep you satisfied and energized between meals.

Snack substitution ideas

1. Veggie sticks for chips

Crunch without the crash—carrots, cucumbers, and peppers with a pinch of salt or a squeeze of lemon.

2. Almonds for candy

Sweet and satisfying—healthy fats + protein help curb cravings and keep you fuller longer.

3. Greek yogurt for ice cream

Creamy and protein-packed—add fruit and a drizzle of honey for dessert vibes.

4. Popcorn for pretzels

Air-popped popcorn delivers volume and fiber for fewer calories—season to taste.

5. Rice cakes for crackers

Whole-grain rice cakes with nut butter, cottage cheese, or avocado keep things light but satisfying.

6. Hummus for creamy dips

Protein-rich and flavorful—pair with veggie sticks or whole-grain pita.

7. Fruit for granola bars

Naturally sweet with fiber and micronutrients—add nuts for staying power.
