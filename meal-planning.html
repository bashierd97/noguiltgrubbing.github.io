Create a weekly template

Step 1: Set your goals

Decide what you’re aiming for (e.g., weight maintenance, strength, more veggies). Keep goals realistic and tied to your week.

Step 2: Choose meal anchors

Plan meals that include:

  • Protein at every meal
  • Veggies with lunch & dinner
  • Healthy fats in moderation
  • Complex carbohydrates

Step 3: Make a simple rotation

Pick 2 breakfasts, 2 lunches, and 3 dinners that you actually enjoy. Rotate them Mon–Fri to reduce decisions and waste.

Make a shopping list (by aisle)

Sections to use

  • Produce
  • Proteins (meat/eggs/plant)
  • Dry goods
  • Dairy/alternatives
  • Frozen

Pro tips

  • Check pantry/freezer first
  • Buy versatile bases (rice, quinoa, greens)
  • Plan for leftovers as a future meal

Prep & batch workflow

Quick prep wins

  • Pre-chop onions/peppers/carrots
  • Cook a pot of grains for the week
  • Roast a tray of mixed veggies

Batch protein

  • Grill/bake chicken or tofu
  • Brown turkey with taco seasoning
  • Slow-cook beans or lentils

Sample 5-day meal plan

A full Mon–Fri plan you can copy, adapt, or rotate. Each day includes protein at every meal and vegetables at lunch and dinner.

Monday

Breakfast: Oatmeal with mixed berries, walnuts, and a drizzle of honey
Snack: Apple slices + almond butter
Lunch: Grilled chicken salad — mixed greens, cherry tomatoes, cucumber, olive oil + lemon dressing
Snack: Greek yogurt + a small handful of almonds
Dinner: Baked salmon with quinoa and roasted vegetables (broccoli, zucchini, red pepper)

Tuesday

Breakfast: Greek yogurt parfait with granola, berries, and a drizzle of honey
Snack: Veggie sticks with hummus
Lunch: Turkey and avocado wrap on a whole-grain tortilla with carrot sticks
Snack: Air-popped popcorn
Dinner: Stir-fried tofu with cauliflower rice, edamame, and soy-ginger sauce

Wednesday

Breakfast: Scrambled eggs (2–3) with spinach, mushrooms, and whole-grain toast
Snack: Banana + a small handful of mixed nuts
Lunch: Lentil soup with a side salad (leafy greens, cucumber, olive oil)
Snack: Rice cake with nut butter and sliced banana
Dinner: Baked chicken thighs with sweet potato wedges and steamed green beans

Thursday

Breakfast: Chia pudding (prepped the night before) with sliced mango and coconut flakes
Snack: Cottage cheese with fresh berries
Lunch: Tuna salad (tuna + Greek yogurt + celery + mustard) on whole-grain bread with veggie sticks
Snack: Dark chocolate (2 squares) + a handful of almonds
Dinner: Turkey meatballs in tomato sauce with zucchini noodles and parmesan

Friday

Breakfast: Protein smoothie — spinach, frozen mango, Greek yogurt, almond milk, chia seeds
Snack: Edamame with sea salt
Lunch: Buddha bowl — brown rice, roasted chickpeas, cucumber, avocado, shredded carrot, tahini dressing
Snack: Fresh fruit with a small handful of walnuts
Dinner: Salmon tacos in lettuce wraps — baked salmon, cabbage slaw, Greek yogurt sauce, lime

Make it work for you

  • Repeat breakfasts: Rotate just 2–3 options Mon–Fri to reduce morning decisions
  • Batch cook Sunday: Grill proteins, cook a pot of grains, and roast a tray of veg — that covers 3–4 dinners
  • Flexible dinners: Keep Wed/Thu dinners as "leftovers or freestyle" nights to reduce planning pressure
