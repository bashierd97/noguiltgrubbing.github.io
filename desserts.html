Healthier dessert substitutions

Indulge your sweet tooth with smart swaps that keep the flavor and dial up the nutrition.

Dessert substitution ideas

1. Avocado chocolate mousse

Blend ripe avocado, cocoa powder, a pinch of salt, and a touch of maple or dates for silky, dairy-free mousse.

2. Chia seed pudding for creamy desserts

Stir chia into almond or dairy milk with vanilla; chill overnight. Add fruit for fiber + omega-3s.

3. Banana “nice” cream for ice cream

Blend frozen bananas (and a splash of milk) until soft-serve smooth. Optional: peanut butter or cocoa.

4. Whole-wheat flour for white flour

Swap 50–100% in muffins and quick breads to boost fiber and nutrients without losing tenderness.

5. Natural nut butters for frosting

Whisk peanut or almond butter with a little honey and yogurt for a creamy, protein-rich topping.

6. Fresh fruit for candy

Go for fruit or warm compotes—sweetness with vitamins, antioxidants, and water content.

7. Oatmeal cookies for traditional cookies

Use oats + mashed banana or applesauce to reduce added sugar and add chew with more fiber.
