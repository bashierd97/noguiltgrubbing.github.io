Baked Salmon with Roasted Vegetables

Ingredients:

  • 2 salmon fillets
  • 2 cups mixed vegetables (broccoli, carrots, bell peppers)
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper

Instructions:

Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C). Toss vegetables with olive oil and seasonings. Place salmon and vegetables on a baking sheet. Bake for 15-20 minutes until salmon is cooked through.
