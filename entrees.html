Healthier entree substitutions

Small swaps, big payoff—keep the comfort while adding nutrients, fiber, and balance to your favorite mains.

Entree substitution ideas

1. Cauliflower for rice

Much lower in calories and carbs than white rice, with bonus fiber and micronutrients. Great under stir-fries or curries.

2. Zucchini noodles for pasta

Lighten pasta dishes by swapping in zoodles for part or all of the noodles. Toss with marinara or pesto and a protein.

3. Cauliflower steaks for meat

Roast thick slices with olive oil, garlic, and spices. A hearty, plant-forward center that takes on flavor beautifully.

4. Chickpeas for ground meat

Mash and season for tacos, sloppy joes, or patties. Protein + fiber = filling and budget-friendly.

5. Portobellos for burger patties

Grilled portobello caps deliver a meaty texture with fewer calories. Load with your usual burger toppings.

6. Sweet potatoes for white potatoes

More vitamin A and fiber. Use for baked wedges, mash, or as a base for bowls with protein and veggies.

7. Greek yogurt for sour cream

Same tangy creaminess, more protein and less fat. Perfect in sauces, dressings, and dolloped on chilis or tacos.
